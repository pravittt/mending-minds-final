GET HELP

If you or someone you know is struggling with gambling addiction in Toronto, there are several resources available to provide support and assistance:

The Centre for Addiction and Mental Health (CAMH): CAMH is a leading mental health and addiction treatment center in Toronto. They offer a variety of resources for gambling addiction, including individual counseling, group therapy, and specialized treatment programs.

Contact CAMH at 416-535-8501 or visit their website at: https://www.camh.ca/en/health-info/mental-illness-and-addiction-index/problem-gambling

Gamblers Anonymous (GA): GA is a self-help group that provides support to individuals who are struggling with gambling addiction. They offer regular meetings in Toronto and around the world.

Visit their website at: https://www.gamblersanonymous.org/ga/locations/canada/toronto

The Responsible Gambling Council (RGC): RGC is a non-profit organization that provides information and resources to individuals, families, and communities affected by gambling addiction. They offer a variety of services, including counseling, support groups, and educational resources.

Visit their website at: https://www.responsiblegambling.org

The Ontario Problem Gambling Helpline: The helpline provides confidential support and information to individuals who are struggling with gambling addiction.

Call 1-888-230-3505 or visit their website at: https://www.connexontario.ca/Addiction-Services/Problem-Gambling

The Gambling Research Exchange Ontario (GREO): GREO is an organization that provides research and knowledge exchange services related to gambling addiction. They offer a variety of resources, including research articles, educational resources, and a directory of gambling treatment services in Ontario.

Visit their website at: https://www.greo.ca
